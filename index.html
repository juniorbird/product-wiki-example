Tag Pages Press Release

Mission Statement

Tag pages replace microsites with customizable pages that bring free traffic and content; and help users find more of the content they're already reading.

Why

Microsites have several weaknesses:

  • No organic traffic
  • Specialized microsite content doesn't continue to exist on Site
  • Site content doesn't flow to microsites

At the same time, Site has a big challenge: we're selling entertainment and game launch ad packages well, because these are short and tied to a specific date. We don't sell ads for cars, razors, etc. well because those are long and shouldn't be tied to a specific event.

Moving microsites to Site as Tag Pages will:

  • Get us organic traffic
  • Keep the microsite content -- which is usually very good -- on Site
  • Keep the tag updated, because new content flows right into it
  • Give us brand new inventory to sell to brand new customers in CPG, QSR, automotive, and more.

Measurable Outcomes

  • All sales-related

Non-Measurable Outcomes

  • Sales feels the tool is adequate
  • SI can launch desired integrations on them
  • Editors find the tool useful

Wireframes

Tagged person

A tagged person home page has links to other tagged pages, a banner to show that it's tagged, and can have the video full-page-width at the top. A tagged event page is similar.

Tagged article

A tagged article has a banner to show that it's in the tag, and tools to discover and navigate to other tagged articles

Tag home page

The home page is a place to discover all of the content under a tag.

Basic tag home page

A tag home page doesn't need much on it; at its base, it just brings all the content together in one place! Most tag pages will probably look like this.

Tag home page with featured articles

Just like the site home page has a featured article list that editors can add articles to and set the order of, tag pages should have that too. The list of recent articles shown above goes below, here.

Tag home page with grid-style featured articles

Editors can choose to have featured articles on a tag home page, just like on the site home page. They can also choose to display those in a grid!

General Requirements

  • Editor capability to add a new tag
  • High-impact design elements on the page, so that a tag home page looks unique and is sponsorable
  • Design elements that visually link tagged pages and drive traffic between pages within a tag
  • Short URLs that we can give to advertisers, so that they can feel special; and for SEO purposes.

What's Different on Mobile?

Nothing significant:

  • Focus on top-to-bottom navigation
  • Some parts of mobile can be delivered after October 1, while the desktop part needs to precede that.

Stories to Create Tag Capabilities

Minimum Viable Product

  • AZ 702: Editors can create tags
  • AZ 703: Articles, persons, and events can be tagged
  • AZ 708: Tags have home pages
  • AZ 706: Tagged articles have a banner at the top

First-Priority Enhancements

  • AZ 709: Ads can be targeted to tagged pages
  • AZ 704: Tagged articles have More on Tag box replace "Related Articles"
  • AZ 705: Tagged articles have Next in Tag and Previously in Tag box

Minimum Requirements for Upcoming Watch

Due Date: October 1!

  • AZ 733: URL Slug is renamed Short URL (To fix confusion)
  • AZ 707: Editors can make person videos run at the top of a tagged Finder profile page
  • AZ 718: Tag home pages can have videos placed in leader area
  • AZ 711: Tag pages can have featured articles
  • AZ 720: SI can suppress tag banners
  • AZ 734: Upcoming Watch has a menubar link

Desirable for Upcoming Watch Launch

  • AZ 736: Mobile Finder events & profiles display tag items
  • AZ 737: Mobile articles, features, etc. display tag items
  • AZ 724: Tag banners appear on Mobile
  • AZ 717: Mobile Finder has videos (provides in-line links to videos in Finder)

Post-Upcoming Watch-Launch Priorities

  • AZ 725: Editors can set tag page precedence
  • AZ 724: Tag banners appear on Mobile
  • AZ 720: Tag pages can be promoted in empty space in menubar
  • AZ 710: Tag home pages can have elements placed in leader area
  • AZ 712: Tag page featured articles can be "Grid-Style"
  • AZ 715: Editors can suppress Forum threads box on tagged pages
  • AZ 740: Tagged items support multiple tags
